2. Communication

Key Opinion Leader (KOL) is very useful for new brand. But how to fix an appropriate one?

I and Philippe created three personas representing target customers.

Cindy
Freshman in College
My teeth look not good. I really want to be prettier to make friends and attract cute boys. I want to align my teeth. I am afraid that mental braces look embarrassing. But I also know Invisalign is so expensive.
Serena
Senior Office Lady
My teeth are fine. I heard that treatment can improve my appearance somehow. I have enough money so I want to have a try. But I am too busy to go to dentist. Especially I heard I need to wait dentist quite often.
Wallace
Big Data Engineer
My teeth are crowded. I am wondering if that is the reason I don‚Äôt have a grilfriend. I want to do treatment with my own money. And I have that amount. I love new technology and product.

According to what personas need, we focused on two types of KOL. They were about FASHION and HEALTH. So I talked with these KOL's assistants via Wechat. It was an interesting process. One did not believe in the product because her dentists' words. Some just ignored my message after I introduced our brand. More surprisingly, one agreed straightforward but she turned out to be fake.

After searching and talking, we finally chose Queen - Yudian.

We talked together and came to an conclusion about the communication stragedy. Yudian would use our product by herself and pubulish two articles and six videos:

  • The first article introduces the product and teach readers how to make impressions.
  • Six videos would go through her treatment process to show people the feeling of being aligned.
  • After finishing the treatment, the final article would tell the effects of the product.
