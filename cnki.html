China National Knowledge Infrastructure
Numerous essays for students and scholars to search and read online


CNKI is knowledge platform providing scholars with Chinese academic and foreign literature, newspapers, conferences and other resources. There is also specific fonctions like paper reviewing, etc.

I took part in mainly three parts of the projects: detail page design, web map design and design standard making.

1. Detail Page Design

Main function of this page is to show detailed information of the literature including author and abstract.I improved the presentation of information and guided users to compare certain literature with related ones.

1.1 User Research

Target users of CNKI I interviewed :

Two conclusions I drawn from interview: one was users’ browing and operation process in the page.

The other was opinions towards comparing certain literature with related ones.

  • Nearly Half users thought they would compare literatures with related ones.
  • Users preferred to compare titile, abstract and key words.

1.2.1 Pain Point of Previous Version

1.2.2 Improvements

  • Omit the rest if “funds” number is larger than two. Show the number cause it is a referable data.
  • Keep “key words” behind “abstract”, in front of “fonds”.
  • Put operation options together, show important ones and omit others.

1.3 New Function: Literature Comparation

User's need:

Users noramlly search for more than one literature on a certain topic for one time. They also want to have related literatures in the page.

How do users select the literatures they want? - By comparing related ones with that certain one.

What do users refer when comparing? - “Title” , “abstract” and “key words”.

Solutions:

  • When users compare, the literature with related information floats on the upper.
  • When users don’t compare, they can minimize the floating part.

2. Website Map Design

Except resources, CNKI provides various services, such as Duplicate Checking System and Academic Forum. In order to let users get to where they want conveniently, a website map is necessary.

I and a senior student first listed all services in mind map and classified them. Then we finished the map with PhotoShop.

Sectional Mind Map of CNKI Services

According to the mind map, we made 3 designs for webmap and there is my favorite one. We use blue rectangle and blue words on the left to show users where they are. Everytime blue rectangle moves to next part, we can see the content of that part occupies most of the screen. And the little grey circle beside turns orange.
