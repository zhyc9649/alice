Happy Papercut
Android App for children to cut paper and play with stickers


Happy Papercut is an Android project for children to play with. The project will not launch in app store. Instead, it will be used as an tool for experiment.

Our RD team consisted of 4 people, and I did mainly two parts, "my profile" and "play with stickers".

2.1 Choosing stickers and changing their position, size, direction.

2.2 Saving or locking or reseting the painting.
